Sudan is as beautiful as the Nile River that runs through it. There are beautiful underwater worlds in the Red Sea and wildlife-rich savannas in Dinder. This land is full of places for you to explore. You are welcome to go on a trip into its deep and hear the stories of this old land.


Meroe

Did you know that sudan actully have more pyramid than eygpt, this stunning site has more than 200 pyramids from the Napata Kingdom. Visitors can step back in time and tour the ruins in the middle of the Sudanese desert.


Kerma

The Western Deffufa is one of the biggest mud-brick buildings in the world, and the ruins of Kerma tell stories from over 5,000 years ago, making it a must-see for history buffs.


Jebel Barkal

Jabel Barkalis is a holy mountain with many ruins and churches at its base. It gives you a unique look into the past and beautiful views of the area.


Dinder national park

People who like animals can see many kinds of animals, like lions and ostriches, in their natural environment at this park.


Sanganeb National Park

The coral reefs in Sanganeb National Park, which is in the Red Sea, make it a famous marine haven. Sanganeb is the only island in the Red Sea, and its clear seas and wide range of coral make it a great place to dive.
